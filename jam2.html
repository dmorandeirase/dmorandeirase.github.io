Fishs & Cheese

3D Farming Game

Description

If you like to work and get robbed, you won't like this game. Catch fish with bubbles and feed the empire of a beaver that only wants more money so you get more fish, and so you make more money, and more fish ... Well you know, to catch fish that the beaver does not get rich alone.
In this game you will help a mouse to catch different kinds of slippery fish with the help of it's bubble wand and sell them to keep advancing in the game.

This game was developed in the Global Game Jam 2025 by 3 programmers and 3 artist and you can download it completely free in this LINK.
