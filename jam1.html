Slave of the Joke

Platformer 2D Game

Description

Slave of the joke is a 2D platform pixel art game that focuses on fast and fluid movement. We are set in a medieval era, where we play the court jester. Her job implies that she has to overcome different deadly tests and make the king laugh in order to keep her position and her life.

This game was developed in the Global Game Jam 2024 by 3 programmers, 1 artist, 1 designer and 1 musician.

C# Unity
