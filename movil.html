LinkUp

2D Puzzle Game

Description

This game is inspired by the mechanics of Atomas, but instead of combining atoms, the player merges numbers. The player starts with a circular ring containing small numbers, and each turn must place a new number or a special orb into the circle. When two identical numbers are adjacent and a plus orb is used betwwen them, they merge into a single number of double the value, freeing up space and pushing the sequence forward.
The goal is to strategically grow numbers to higher values while managing limited space. Special orbs, like plus and minus, add tactical depth by allowing players to combine or reposition numbers to set up chain reactions. The game challenges players to plan ahead, create combos, and prevent the ring from filling up, making for a minimalist but deeply strategic puzzle experience.
